ThingzApp

Build Your Perfect IoT App: Flexible and Customizable with ThingzApp

ThingzApp empowers you to seamlessly connect, collect, and analyze data from your IoT devices. Our highly customizable mobile app simplifies complex workflows and delivers powerful insights, accelerating your journey towards data-driven decision making.

By enabling quick, localized processing of IoT data, NearThingz speeds up the transformation of large data streams into actionable insights, significantly boosting response times and improving operational efficiency.

Key Features

Immense Integration Capability

ThingzApp integrates effortlessly with various cloud platforms, databases, and CRM/ERP systems, ensuring compatibility with your existing infrastructure.

Actionable Data Analytics

The ThingzApp not only shows you the raw data being streamed by your devices for deeper insights but also seamlessly deciphers and visualizes this data. This enables you to interpret your data with ease and facilitates actionable data analytics. Visualize and analyze sensor data to gain valuable insights into your operations, empowering you to make informed decisions based on real-time and historical data.

Offline Data Safety

ThingzApp functions seamlessly even in areas with limited or no connectivity. Data is stored locally and uploaded automatically when a network connection is established.

Immense Connectivity Capabilities

ThingzApp shatters connectivity barriers. Connect virtually any external hardware, regardless of manufacturer or protocol (BLE, RFID, NFC, and more). Our versatile solution over-simplifies the ever- annoying device integration procedures.

Data Collection Capabilities

Every device speaks a unique data language. ThingzApp acts as your expert translator, effortlessly deciphering messages from various hardware vendors and protocols. This allows you to focus on extracting insights, not deciphering data formats.

Custom Workflows

We understand that every industry and use case presents unique challenges. ThingzApp's highly customizable platform empowers you to create bespoke customer journeys within your mobile app with minimal coding, often within weeks.

Ready to Use Industry Specific Workflows

ThingzApp goes beyond a one-size-fits-all approach. We offer ready-to-use industry specific mobile app solutions tailored to address the specific needs of various industries, including:

  • Manufacturing
  • Healthcare
  • Automotive
  • Infrastructure & more
